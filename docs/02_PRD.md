# 1. Executive Summary

RetailPilot AI is an AI-powered Retail Business Intelligence Platform designed specifically for small and medium electronics retail businesses.

The platform enables retailers to upload sales datasets and automatically receive business insights, dashboards, predictive analytics, inventory recommendations, customer segmentation, and sales forecasts.

Instead of manually analyzing spreadsheets, business owners can make smarter business decisions using Data Science and Machine Learning.

The MVP focuses on Electronics Retail Stores, while the long-term vision is to expand into a universal retail intelligence platform supporting multiple business domains.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Most electronics retailers store business data in Excel spreadsheets.

Although valuable information exists within these datasets, business owners often cannot answer questions such as:

* Which products generate maximum profit?
* Which products should be discontinued?
* Which customers are most valuable?
* Which cities generate maximum revenue?
* Which inventory should be increased?
* How will next month's sales perform?
* Why are sales decreasing?

Hiring Data Analysts or Business Intelligence experts is expensive for small businesses.

Existing BI tools are often difficult to use and require technical expertise.

---

# 3. Proposed Solution

RetailPilot AI provides an easy-to-use platform that automatically converts raw retail sales data into meaningful business intelligence.

The platform performs:

* Data Validation
* Data Cleaning
* Exploratory Data Analysis
* Customer Segmentation
* Sales Forecasting
* Product Analysis
* Inventory Recommendations
* Business Health Scoring
* AI Business Insights
* Downloadable Reports

The platform focuses on helping retailers make business decisions instead of simply displaying charts.

* Category Analysis
* Heatmaps

## Customer Segmentation

Cluster customers using Machine Learning.

Categories:

* Premium
* Loyal
* Regular
* Inactive

## Sales Forecasting

Predict:

* Next Month
* Next Quarter

---

## Product Intelligence

Identify:

* Worst Selling Products

* Missing values.
