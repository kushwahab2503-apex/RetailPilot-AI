# RetailPilot AI

## Project Vision Document

**Version:** 1.0 (MVP)

**Project Type:** AI-Powered Retail Business Intelligence Platform

**Project Status:** Planning Phase

**Developed By:** Badal Kushwaha

---

# Vision Statement

RetailPilot AI aims to empower small and medium electronics retail businesses by transforming raw sales data into meaningful business insights through Data Science, Machine Learning, Predictive Analytics, and Artificial Intelligence.

Instead of relying on spreadsheets and manual calculations, business owners will be able to upload their sales data and instantly understand how their business is performing, what products generate the highest profit, which customers are most valuable, what inventory should be restocked, and what actions should be taken to improve overall business performance.

Our vision is to make advanced business intelligence simple, affordable, and accessible for every retailer.

---

# Mission Statement

Our mission is to build an intelligent business analytics platform that enables retailers to make faster, smarter, and data-driven decisions without requiring technical knowledge of Data Science.

RetailPilot AI will automate data cleaning, visualization, forecasting, customer analysis, and business recommendations inside one modern platform.

---

# Problem Statement

Thousands of small electronics retailers maintain sales records in Excel sheets or CSV files.

Although they possess valuable business data, they often struggle to answer important questions such as:

* Which products generate maximum profit?
* Which products should be discontinued?
* Which customers are most valuable?
* Which city performs best?
* How much inventory should be purchased next month?
* Why are sales decreasing?
* What actions can improve revenue?

Most existing business intelligence tools are either too expensive, too complex, or require technical expertise.

---

# Proposed Solution

RetailPilot AI provides a single intelligent platform where retailers can upload their sales data and automatically receive:

* Cleaned datasets
* Interactive dashboards
* Business KPIs
* Customer segmentation
* Sales forecasting
* Inventory recommendations
* Product performance analysis
* Business Health Score
* AI-generated business insights
* Downloadable reports

The objective is not just to analyze data but to help retailers make smarter business decisions.
